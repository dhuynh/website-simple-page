About Duy

Duy Huynh is a student at the University of Waterloo doing his undergrad in Systems Design Engineering. His most recent co-op work term was at a fast moving startup named toushay. On the side, he enjoys tinkering around with hardware and brainstorming ideas. He's even named his arduino Deezed, which means strong and represents Duy's stance on project development. He has delved into the realm of self made projects, software development, and mechanical design. He worked in the power industry before going to University and had his first taste of crunch time when customer orders came in too fast at Absopulse Electronics.

His artsy side includes writing a creative blog at Written Experiment, learning dance at the University of Waterloo Hip Hop club, and full body conditioning so he can try his hand at breakdancing. He plays guitar, flute and enjoys competitive gaming. See his steam profile at his tag namDa. 'Nam' means south in his native tongue while namDa together is a play on lambda, a greek symbol that represents many things in the physical world. He likes to interpret his tag as southern connection, but he's not sure if he's convinced anybody of that. Oh, one last thing. Have you noticed the logo at the top right of his website is his initials as well as a wine glass? Classy.
